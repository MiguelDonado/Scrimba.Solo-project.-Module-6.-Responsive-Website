Fragas do Eume, a pristine forest in Galicia, Spain, spans 9,000 hectares along the Eume River. This ancient woodland, resilient against time and fire, boasts towering oaks and diverse wildlife. Its trails lead to historic monasteries, like Caaveiro, offering a serene escape into nature's embrace. The Fragas do Eume stands as a testament to the harmonious coexistence of preservation and the environment, inviting visitors to immerse themselves in its timeless beauty.
